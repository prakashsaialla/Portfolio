Just for the Data of It

Because analysts analyze everything - including themselves

Spent 3 hours debugging a model only to discover I'd been training on the test set. The model was great. My confidence was not.

Built a Spotify listening dashboard just for fun - turns out I listen to the same 3 playlists on a loop. Variety score: 0.04 out of 10. Comfort score: 10 out of 10.

Ran a linear regression on study hours vs. grades last semester - R² = 0.81. Effort explains 81% of outcomes. The other 19% is clearly Wi-Fi quality.

Tracked sleep hours vs. next day productivity. Below 6 hours, my StackOverflow search rate triples. Data doesn't lie - but sleep deprived me absolutely does.

During deadline week my browser hits 30+ open tabs. Around 10 are actual docs, 8 are StackOverflow and at least 3 are the exact same page opened twice.

Built a full Tinder & Bumble analytics dashboard after running out of ideas - tracked swipe rates, match ratios, and response times. The data was conclusive: the algorithm needs work. So does my bio.
